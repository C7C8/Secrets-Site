How should I interpret these results?

Don't worry if your secrets aren't completely identical, that's extremely rare! It just means that maybe your partner didn't use the exact same wording, that's all.

You should pay special attention to the keyword analysis -- it's a primitive analysis, but if you used keyword-heavy secrets it should be a good indicator of how much of your information matches up with your partner's. Briefly, "overlap" is when your partner's secret has an occurrence of the same word yours has; the overlap percentage is the fraction of overlapped words vs. the total number of distinct words used. So, if you both dropped a certain name or key word, that would show up as keyword overlap.

Oh, and in case you're worried about your secrets getting out -- don't worry, they've already been deleted.
